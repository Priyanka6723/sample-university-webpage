Our Certificate & Online Program

The College has a modern infrastructure in a 44-acre campus with different blocks containing Smart Classrooms, Conference Hall, Auditorium with full house capacity of 2500, 3 Seminar Halls with a capacity of 300, 200 and 100), Library and other facilities. The Central Library, fully computerized, has resources in the form of books, non-book materials and journals in print and in e-form. The collection includes 6287 titles with 38170 volumes of books, 142 journals and 4797 Digital Resources. The Library is extensively used by the students and faculty. The hostels have several new blocks, with well-furnished rooms. There are separate hostels for Men and Women Boarders. The hostels have spacious reading rooms provided with newspapers, weeklies, monthlies, other valuable reading material, T.V. sets, computers, water purifiers and Solar Water-Heaters. The hostels are also provided with a Gymnasium, WiFi and facilities for Indoor games as well as guest rooms for visiting parents. There is a vast sports arena capable of staging Zonal and Inter-Zonal Competitions. A well equipped Gymnasium and courts for indoor and outdoor games are available. The students trained by qualified and experienced Physical Directors have won Trophies and Shields in several Zonal, Inter-Zonal and National-level Tournaments. The other facilities include Wi-Fi Facility, Campus Internet Connectivity, 75 Mbps 1:1 Leased-line connectivity and a full-fledged Post Office within the campus. Besides, there are amenities like two canteens providing delicious dishes both Vegetarian and Non-Vegetarian, Cafeteria, City Union Bank Branch with 24 hours global ATM facility and student stationery stall. A Health Care Centre manned by a well-qualified doctor, assisted by a trained nurse takes care of the health of students.
